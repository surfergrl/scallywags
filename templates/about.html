More about me and my business

I have always loved animals, and studied and worked as a wildlife filmographer. I have worked for the BBC's Natural History unit, including spending time in Africa filming environmental documentaries.

When I finished my MA in Film Studies at Bristol University, I decided to train as a dog walker to use my skills and indulge my love of dogs, while still freelancing in film and editing.

Dog care has become my full-time job; way beyond financial rewards, this career offers me flexibility, independence and the joy of working with animals every day.

If you’re looking for a trustworthy, qualified, and passionate dog walker or pet sitter, I’m here to help. Let’s work together to ensure your pets get the care and attention they deserve.

If you have any questions at all, please do get in touch!

What I Offer

  • Dog Walking: Whether it’s a brisk walk in the park or a leisurely stroll, I make sure your dog gets the exercise they need to thrive. I cater to all breeds, sizes, and energy levels.
  • Sitting: When you’re away, I’ll be there to care for your pets in the comfort of their own home. I provide feeding, playtime, and companionship to keep them content.
  • Pet Boarding: Safe and loving care in my home.
  • Pet Taxi: Reliable transportation for vet visits or grooming appointments.
  • Grooming: Bathing, brushing, and basic grooming services to keep your pet looking their best.

My Qualifications

I am committed to the highest standards of pet care. My qualifications include:

  • Certification in Canine First Aid Level 2 (VTQ) from the British College of Canine Studies.
  • Certification in Animal Behaviour: An Introduction from the University of Oxford.
  • I am a member of the National Association of Registered Pet Sitters (NARPS).
  • I have an enhanced DBS check which can be seen on request.
